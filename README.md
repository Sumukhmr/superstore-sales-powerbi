# Superstore Sales Dashboard | Power BI

An interactive Sales Operations dashboard built 
in Power BI using the popular Superstore dataset.

---

## Dashboard Overview

A comprehensive sales analytics dashboard covering
key business metrics across regions, segments,
categories, and shipping modes.

---

## KPIs Tracked

- Total Sales
- Total Quantity Sold
- Average Delivery Days
- Monthly Sales YoY
- Monthly Profit YoY

---

## Visuals Included

- Sales by Customer Segment (Pie chart)
- Monthly Sales Year over Year (Line chart)
- Monthly Profit Year over Year (Line chart)
- Sales by Ship Mode (Bar chart)
- Sales by Category (Bar chart)
- Sales by Sub Category (Bar chart)
- Sales and Profit by State (Map visual)
- Payment Mode breakdown (Pie chart)
- Region slicer for interactive filtering

---

## Tools Used

- Power BI Desktop
- Dataset: Superstore Sales (public dataset)
